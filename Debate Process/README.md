## 🧭 Debate Process
Due to the double-blind review requirements, our Google Drive containing the full debate logs cannot be made publicly available at this stage. As the complete set of debate processes is large in scale, we are unable to release all debate transcripts at once. To provide concrete insights into the debate dynamics, we present a partial case study here using **Qwen3-4B** in the **biology** domain. For each MBTI personality pairing, we release two representative debate examples for illustration.
